AH Packages: From Trading to Leading Corrugated Box Manufacturer

The story of AH Packages is one of perseverance, partnership, and a deep commitment to quality in the corrugated packaging industry. From custom corrugated boxes to wholesale corrugated boxes, AH Packages has grown from humble beginnings to become Pakistan's trusted corrugated box manufacturer and packaging supplier for custom printed boxes, shipping boxes, and sustainable packaging solutions.

AH Packages Humble Beginnings (2005–2006)

Two brothers—my father and his brother—founded AH Packages as a trading business, supplying reliable corrugated boxes, cardboard boxes, and shipping boxes to local businesses. Without a factory of their own, AH Packages mastered quality control, timely delivery, and service by working closely with manufacturing units and customers. This foundation revealed what customers truly value in a packaging supplier, and AH Packages built its reputation on delivering affordable custom packaging and bulk corrugated boxes.

AH Packages: Building Our Foundation, Piece by Piece

The goal was always control over quality and customization. Step by step, AH Packages invested in machinery—corrugation, printing, die‑cutting—moving from traders to true manufacturers of custom corrugated boxes, die-cut boxes, and custom printed boxes. Each machine was a hard‑won investment and a commitment to better products and service for AH Packages customers seeking wholesale corrugated boxes and custom packaging solutions.

  1. AH Packages Corrugation Capability

    AH Packages core strength and board control unlocked consistent performance for single wall corrugated, double wall corrugated, and triple wall corrugated boxes with E flute, C flute, and B flute options.

  2. AH Packages Printing Expansion

    AH Packages improved branding with accurate, repeatable color and registration for custom printed cardboard boxes, logo-printed shipping boxes, and branded packaging design.

  3. AH Packages Die‑Cutting & Finishing

    AH Packages greater design freedom and tighter tolerances at scale for custom die-cut packaging, RSC boxes, and retail-ready packaging solutions.

AH Packages: The Realization of a Dream

Years of dedication and reinvestment led to AH Packages own factory unit, bringing the full production roadmap under one roof—from raw materials to the final custom corrugated boxes, shipping boxes, and cardboard boxes. This enabled the strict cost‑effective and high‑quality standards AH Packages upholds today for wholesale corrugated boxes and bulk corrugated boxes.

AH Packages has grown from two brothers trading boxes into a comprehensive, modern manufacturing unit—guided by humility, reliability, and respect for our customers and our people. Today, AH Packages stands as Pakistan's trusted leader in corrugated packaging solutions, offering everything from custom printed boxes and e-commerce shipping boxes to sustainable packaging solutions and industrial corrugated containers.
